    SamRIBot is a Twitch.tv Chat Bot running on the PhantomBot Platform. It's primary objective is to make viewers of the SamRItv Twitch Channel feel welcomed and comfortable while in chat.

    Its secondary objective is to provide Viewers with points, or Yen, for being loyal to the stream. Yen can be redeemed for many prizes, such as Shiny Battle- and/or Mega-ready Pokémon for use in Pokémon Ultra sun, Ultra Moon, Sun, or Moon. Also, once you have enough time in chat, earned enough Yen, and you have acquired Regular status, you may apply to be a Moderator
